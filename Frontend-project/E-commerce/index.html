Features

Let's go beyond your imagination.

Built on NVIDIA’s Ada Lovelace architecture, the RTX 4090 provides significant performance improvements over the previous Ampere architecture. It brings enhanced efficiency, AI capabilities, and ray tracing.

The RTX 4090 is equipped with 16,384 CUDA cores, which dramatically improves parallel processing power, allowing faster rendering times and higher frame rates.

With a power rating of 450W TDP, the RTX 4090 requires a robust power supply (NVIDIA recommends at least 850W) and adequate cooling. Most cards come with advanced cooling systems to handle the thermal load effectively.
